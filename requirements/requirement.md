# Sell Shoes Online with MVP 

What we are selling: Shoes
Where: Online Platform
Why im building this: To understand system design, Product lifecycle, Secure Design.


What Do customers want?
1. Buy Shoes
2. Find Shoes
3. Compare Shoes
4. Check size, color, availability
5. Pay
6. Receive Delivery


## 1. Functional Requirements

1. Users can Register
2. Users can Login
3. Users can Logout
4. Users can Search Products
5. Users can Compare the Shoes
6. Users can check the size, color availability of the shoes
7. Users can add/remove shoes to cart
8. Users can add/remove shoes to wishlist/Favorites
9. Users can place order
10. Users can pay the amount
11. Users can contact/mail for queries, newletters etc
